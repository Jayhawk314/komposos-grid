## 1. Why Five Layers?

### The Separation of Concerns

Each layer solves a **different problem**:

| Layer | Problem | Solution |
|-------|---------|----------|
| **Orion** | "How do I add capabilities?" | Hot-loadable plugins |
| **KOMPOSOS-IV** | "How do I ensure correctness?" | Category-theoretic laws |
| **∞-Cosmos** | "How do I reason about higher structure?" | 2-cells, fibrations, Yoneda |
| **COG** | "How do I reason efficiently?" | Tiered verification |
| **OPTIMUS** | "How do I improve myself?" | Categorical gradient descent |

**None of these can replace the others** -- they're orthogonal concerns.

The first three layers give you a production AI agent. The fourth makes it **self-improving**. The fifth (∞-Cosmos + Dual Engine) makes it **self-aware**.
