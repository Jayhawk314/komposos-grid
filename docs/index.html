Read this before quoting numbers

Measured numbers (seam spreads, corridor values) come from grid operators' own hourly settlement data times observed flows. Screening numbers (B/C brackets, assumed O&M) are honest estimates with stated assumptions — good enough to rank options, not to commit money. Relief is always capped at the congestion that actually exists; the two sides of one border are never double-counted. Full trust ledger: MASTER_GUIDE §6.
